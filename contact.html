Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for destinations?

Simply use the search bar at the top of any page and type in the city name you're interested in. Our system will provide you with photos, weather information, and other relevant details.

Are the weather updates real-time?

Yes! We use the OpenWeatherMap API to provide current weather conditions and forecasts for destinations worldwide, updated in real-time.

Where do the destination photos come from?

All destination photos are sourced from Unsplash API, ensuring high-quality, professional images that showcase the beauty of each location.

Can I save my favorite destinations?

Currently, your recent searches are automatically saved in your browser's local storage. We're working on adding a favorites feature in future updates.

Is the website mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! Exploora is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices, tablets, and desktops, ensuring a great experience on any device.
