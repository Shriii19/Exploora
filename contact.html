Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Find instant answers to common questions about our platform and services

How do I search for destinations?

Simply use the search bar at the top of any page and type in the city name you're interested in. Our system will provide you with high-quality photos, real-time weather information, and comprehensive destination details powered by professional APIs.

Are the weather updates real-time?

Yes! We use the OpenWeatherMap API to provide current weather conditions and detailed forecasts for destinations worldwide. All weather data is updated in real-time and includes temperature, humidity, wind speed, and weather conditions.

Where do the destination photos come from?

All destination photos are sourced from Unsplash API, ensuring high-quality, professional images from talented photographers worldwide. This guarantees stunning visuals that accurately showcase the beauty and character of each location.

Can I save my favorite destinations?

Your recent searches are automatically saved in your browser's local storage for quick access. We're actively developing an enhanced favorites system with user accounts, custom collections, and cross-device synchronization in our upcoming release.

Is the website mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! Exploora is fully responsive and optimized for all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Our mobile-first design approach ensures a seamless experience regardless of your screen size or device type.

What technologies power the platform?

Exploora is built with modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript. We integrate with OpenWeatherMap API for weather data and Unsplash API for professional photography, ensuring fast performance and reliable data.
